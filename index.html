Pattern creation station

Inspired from modern drum machines and grooveboxes, Polaris is an Android app allowing you to quickly create patterns anywhere, anytime.

Anywhere, anytime

Because it runs on a device you always carry with you, capture those inspirational moments right from the start, or let yourself experiment quickly some ideas.

Sequence

Sequencing is at the very core of Polaris. For any of the 6 tracks available, use the 4x4 grid to enter steps and trigger notes.

Because 16 steps might not be enough, you can take advantage of the trig conditions to make a note play once every 4 grid for example.

Oh and one more thing, you can modulate some parameters per step via the modulation sequencer.
