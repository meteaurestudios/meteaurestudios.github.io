polaris

Pattern creation station

Create loop based patterns with Polaris using its 6 tracks sequencer. Modulate key parameters per step to generate complex and rich beats from a very simple interface. Take advantage of the 2 instrument types to generate complex bass lines, harsh noise or use the factory included samples at your disposal.
