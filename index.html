Anywhere, anytime

With your device always at hand, capture those inspirational moments right from the start, or let yourself experiment with quick ideas.

Sequence

Sequencing is at the very core of Polaris. For any of the 6 tracks available, use the 4x4 grid to enter steps and trigger notes.

Because 16 steps might not be enough, you can take advantage of the trig conditions to make a note play once every 4 grids for example.

Oh and one more thing, you can modulate several parameters per step via the modulation sequencer.

Design

Take advantage of the 2 instrument types - sample or synth - to generate complex bass lines, harsh noises, or explore the factory sounds that come with the app.

No matter the instrument, shape your sound in detail thanks to the dedicated filter and distortion modules.

Mix

Adjust key parameters for each track like volume, reverb send or filter cutoff - all from a single place - to bring your pattern to life.
